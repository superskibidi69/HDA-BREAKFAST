Our Story

HDA Breakfast was founded in 2025 by three 6th graders with a simple mission: to provide the most figurative breakfast possible. Our journey began in a language arts class, where we created this for a project. Today, we are proud to serve our class with a menu that combines humor and work, using only the freshest ingredients. Whether you're here for our not so famous bacon, stiff pancakes, or smashed orange juice, we attempt to make every meal a goofy one. Thank you for being a skibidi customer!
